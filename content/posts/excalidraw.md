# My thoughts on Excalidraw

## It's amazing

Excalidraw is one of those rare tools which doesn't slow you down when thinking. 
The reason whiteboards are so popular is because although they have their
shortcomings, namely the lack of undo and their limited size is that they
require next to no brain power to operate. Excalidraw manages to do the same, I
am not focussed on the tool, I can use it while talking. 
It's incredibly simple and incredibly reliable and predictable. 
The downside is that it's also fairly limited, but this is one of those cases
where you can't have your cake and eat it too.
